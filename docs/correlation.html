Correlation Findings

For correlation we decided to look at the features of opening price, closing price, open/close difference, high, low, log returns, volatility, and volume traded to see if there is a commonality between the financial assets in terms of any of these. To look at the the correlation between the assets we used Pearson's R and linear regression. The first heatmap is based on the absolute value of the Pearson's R (so the higher the better) and the second heatmap uses the P value from the Pearson's R calculation, and if below .05 indicates that there is a statistically significant relationship between the two assets. Additionally, click on the cells in the Pearson's R heatmap to see the graph of the linear regression between the two assets.
