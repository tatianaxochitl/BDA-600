Introduction

This project aims to look at the financial assets in the forms of stocks and cryptocurrency, their correlations, and make predicitons.

The Financial Assets

Stocks

  • AAPL - Apple Inc.
  • AMZN - Amazon.com, Inc.
  • FB - Meta
  • JPM - JPMorgan Chase & Co.
  • TSLA - Tesla

Crypto

  • ADA - Cardano
  • BTC - Bitcoin
  • DOGE - Dogecoin
  • ETH - Ethereum
  • SOL - Solana

The Methods

Correlation

  • Pearson's R
  • Linear Regression

Volatility Prediction

  • SVR-GARCH
  • ANN-GARCH

Price Prediction

  • ARIMA
